Minimize your hosting expenses!

Almost CMS eliminates the need for servers or maintenance by using compute functions and API's (the JAM stack). Aside of this, Almost CMS minimizes the amount of API requests made to the point were the only time compute functions are executed is when a new article/picture gets uploaded by you or your sites admin/editor.
